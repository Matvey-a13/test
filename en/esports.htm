What Esports Bet Types Can You Make at GGBet?

The action never stops at GGBet online bookmaker! We have 30 different bet types on a variety of different esports games. Here are some esport betting markets worth checking out!

Match Result

The most common way to bet on esports at GGBet online bookmaker is via a Match Result. With this bet, you simply need to predict if Team A or B will win or if the game will end in a draw. If your prediction is correct, you win.

Double Chance

With a Double Chance wager, you bet on Team A or Team B to win, and even if a draw happens, your bet pays out. This bet is similar to a 1X2 wager but offers less risk and lower odds. If you are a risk-averse gambler or are not confident in your team winning then try a Double Chance bet!

Total Maps

In a Total Maps wager, you need to predict how many maps will be played in a game. This wager is typically presented as an over-under.

Most Kills

With a Most Kills wager, you need to bet on which team will record the most kills throughout the match. For example, you could bet on Cloud9 scoring more kills in a CS:GO go when they take on Natus Vincere.

First Blood

Which player or team will record the first kill? Make a correct prediction to win your first blood bet. This wager is extremely popular as it offers quick action and within minutes, you can find out if you won or lost!

Handicap

With handicap betting GG.BET bookmaker places an imaginary point or goal handicap on a team. For example, if Invictus Gaming is playing JD Gaming in a League of Legends match. Invictus Gaming may have a handicap of -1.5. That means if you bet on Invictus Gaming to win, they have to win by 2 or more. Handicap betting is popular because it allows you to increase your returns when betting on favorites.

Parlays

GGBet bookmaker offers the best parlays in the world! With a parlay, you make multiple individual esports wagers and combine them into one bet. If all of your individual bets win then your parlay pays out combined odds. Parlays are high risk because if one of your individual wagers loses, then your parlay is over but they offer huge returns from small stakes.
